⚙️ How to Listen at 0.25× Speed

On each YouTube player, click the ⚙️ Settings icon. It may appear in the top-right or bottom-right corner of the video player.

Select Playback Speed, then choose 0.25×. If your player uses a speed slider, move the slider all the way to the left.

🎧 For the best experience, headphones or earbuds are strongly recommended.

What Are Hidden Audio Finds?

Hidden Audio Finds are unusual sounds, background noises, voices, effects, and audio details that become easier to notice when a video is played at slower speeds. Many of these sounds go completely unnoticed during normal playback because they occur too quickly for most listeners to recognize.

Most discoveries featured on Hidden Audio Finds are reviewed at 0.25× playback speed. Slowing a video down stretches subtle audio details over a longer period of time, making hidden sounds, layered effects, and unexpected audio moments easier to hear.

Headphones or earbuds are strongly recommended because many hidden audio moments are extremely quiet or occur in only one audio channel. Listening through headphones can reveal details that may be difficult to hear through phone speakers, laptop speakers, or noisy environments.

Want to discover your own hidden audio finds? Choose a video, reduce the playback speed to 0.25×, and listen carefully for unusual sounds, patterns, or details. Many videos contain multiple hidden audio moments that become apparent only through slow-speed listening.
