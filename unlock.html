⚙️ How to Listen at 0.25× Speed

On each YouTube player, click the ⚙️ Settings icon. It may appear in the top-right or bottom-right corner of the video player.

Click ⚙️ Settings, select Playback Speed, and choose 0.25×. If your player uses a speed slider instead of a list, simply move the slider all the way to the left to reach 0.25× speed.

The hidden audio is designed to be heard at 0.25× speed.
